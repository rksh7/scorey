About Scorey

Scorey is for strong teams.
Strong teams root for each other
on & off the field.
Scorey brings teams together
even if they are not on the same field.
Scorey makes it super easy to share
what's happening on the field -
with those not on the field.
Scorey ensures a strong team is always together.
Scorey makes strong teams - stronger.

No download, just use

Scorey is a web application. No installation gimmicks, no OS limits. Use from any browser and from any device.


No IM spamming

No hassles of groups, contacts, muting or unmuting. Start when you want, stop when you want.


Separates chatter from what matters

Let's be honest, our mates often go crazy when texting. But scorey filters the score updates from the chatter.


Simultaneous feed to club website

Imagine your club showing your game updates on the club website. Scorey makes it happen, automatically!
